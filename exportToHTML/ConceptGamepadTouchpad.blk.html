13   <field name="COMMENT"/>
14   <comment pinned="true" h="534" w="452">The Sony PS4 gamepad can detect two distinct touches on the central touchpad.
15   Other gamepads with different touchpads may provide mixed results.
16   
17   The touchpads are accessed through the Gamepad blocks.
18   Several new blocks were added to the Gamepad category in FTC SDK Rev 7.
19       TouchpadFinger1     returns true if at least one finger is detected.
20       TouchpadFinger1X   finger 1 X coordinate.  Valid if TouchpadFinger1 is true
21       TouchpadFinger1Y   finger 1 Y coordinate.  Valid if TouchpadFinger1 is true
22       TouchpadFinger2     returns true if a second finger is detected
23       TouchpadFinger2X   finger 2 X coordinate.  Valid if TouchpadFinger2 is true
24       TouchpadFinger2Y   finger 2 Y coordinate.  Valid if TouchpadFinger2 is true
25   
26   Finger touches are reported with an X and Y coordinate in following coordinate system.
27   
28   X is the Horizontal axis, and Y is the vertical axis.
29   The 0,0 origin is at the center of the touchpad.
30   1.0, 1.0 is at the top right corner of the touchpad.
31   -1.0,-1.0 is at the bottom left corner of the touchpad.</comment>
